About

Before moving to East Lansing, I earned a Bachelor of Science in Astrophysics and a Minor in Philosophy from the University of Florida. Even further back, I was born in Michigan but grew up in sunny South Florida. I was raised Modern Orthodox Jewish, and you can often find me on Friday mornings preparing a weekend chulent. When I am not working or speaking to my grandmother on the phone, you can usually find me reading a book by John Steinbeck, cuddling with my baby tabby cat Oliver, or losing countless hours of sleep to another Sid Meier's Civ VI playthrough or trying to beat the newest Assassin's Creed game.

Meir Schochet, 2025
